About Us




Who We Are

We are Cal State LA’s Formula SAE team, Golden Eagle Motorsports. Our team consists of students of several disciplines including engineering, computer science, business and more. We welcome students of any major since this team requires more than just engineering to perform.

When & Where We Meet

We are usually found in the machine shop B-15 underneath the engineering buildings in front of the CSULA baseball field. Our lab is open all the time assuring that there are members from our team or our sister team Baja SAE. Students are welcome to visit us anytime! Visit our "Contact Us" section to speak to the team for any futher questions.

What We Do

Formula SAE is a team of students who develop and produce an open wheel, open cockpit-based formula style vehicle and compete with schools from around the world. Our competition consists of static and dynamic events testing our design, fabrication and reasoning skills compared to other universities. We push our team members to reach higher potentials by taking them through the engineering cycle which begins by creating requirements, then generating concepts, followed by completing detailed designs, prototyping, testing, manufacturing, and performance verification.

Sponsor Us!

As a university team we seek assistance from whoever would like to help us out. We try to fabricate all our parts and components that go into our project in house but there are some processes we cannot perform due to the need of certain on-campus resources aside from what we have. Any contributions would be greatly appreciated including materials, processes or necessities for the team’s goal which is to compete in the Formula SAE Collegiate Series Competition.

If seeking to sponsor we have three tiers of sponsorship:

Eagle

Sponsor gets a feature in all platforms of our social media including but not limited to Instagram, Twitter and Facebook. Along with a feature, the sponsor will receive a total of three (3) mentions on a platform of their choice throughout the competition season, starting from June 24th, 2019 up to mid-June (exact date TBA). Mention dates may be requested according to the sponsor or the team may set the dates and may be distributed amongst the platforms as needed. An example of the shout-outs may be one (1) on Instagram, one (1) in twitter and one (1) on Facebook amounting to threein total. The sponsor will also have a logo of their choice featured on the 2019-2020 competition vehicle nose cone and a large sized logo featured on the “Eagle” section of our school website. Requests and accommodations for any further needs of sponsor can be discussed and agreed upon if necessary. Sponsors will also be invited on to campus to drive our team vehicle and a tour of our workspace. Sponsors will receive a care package with team merchandise.

Falcon

Sponsor gets a feature in a platform of our social media of their choice. Choices consists of the team’s Instagram, Twitter or Facebook. The sponsor may also get up to two (2) mentions on platform(s) of their choice. An example of the shout-outs may be one (1) on Instagram and one (1) in twitter amounting to two (2) in total. The sponsor will also have logo featured on the 2019-2020 competition vehicle side panel and a medium sized logo featured on the “Falcon” section of our school website.

Pelican

Sponsor will get a total of two (2) mentions on a platform of their choice. An example of the mentions may be one (1) on Instagram and one (1) in twitter amounting to two (2) in total. A small sized picture on the “Pelican” section of the school website.
